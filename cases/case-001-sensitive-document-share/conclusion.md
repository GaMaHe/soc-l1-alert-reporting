# Case 002 – Conclusion

## Final Classification
True Positive – Data Policy Violation

## Decision
The alert was escalated to SOC Level 2 for further investigation and coordination with the Compliance team.

## Rationale
- Sensitive HR data shared outside the organization
- External, unidentified recipient
- Explicit violation of corporate data handling policies

## Recommended Action
- Notify Compliance department
- Contact the user to validate intent and context
- Review access and sharing permissions for HR documents
